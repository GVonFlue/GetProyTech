What does ProyTech do? +
Two things, in order. First we build your website the right way — lead gen, SEO, and copy built on buyer psychology. Then we layer AI on top to run your front office: answering leads in seconds, following up automatically, and connecting all your tools into one system.
Do you only work with realtors and lenders? +
That's where we start, because our founder is a working realtor who lived these exact problems. The same system works for any local business losing calls and leads.
Will this replace my current CRM? +
No. We build on top of the tools you already use and connect them together. We're adding the front office — not another login to babysit.
How does the newsletter help? +
Most of your future clients aren't ready today. A custom-branded newsletter keeps you in their inbox every month with real value — no selling — so when they're finally ready, you're the name they already trust.
What is the monthly fee actually for? +
The setup builds it; the monthly runs it. That covers hosting, SSL and security, updates and monitoring, the AI usage itself, monthly tuning as we learn what's converting, edits whenever you text them in, and real human support. It's not a maintenance fee for a file sitting on a server — it's a system that's actively working every day. And it's month to month: no contract, cancel anytime.
Why a setup fee AND a monthly? +
Because they pay for two different things. The setup is the build — design, copy, SEO, wiring it into your tools, training the AI on your voice. That's real work with a finish line. The monthly is everything that happens after: keeping it running, improving it, and supporting you. We'd rather charge honestly for both than hide one inside the other and mark it up.
How fast can we go live? +
Websites can turn around in days. A front desk setup is usually live within a week or two. The free audit gives you an exact timeline and honest answers.
