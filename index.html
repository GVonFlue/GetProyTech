What does ProyTech actually do? +
Two things. We build premium websites that make you look legit, and we install an AI front desk that answers every call, text, and web lead in seconds — qualifying them and booking appointments 24/7, all wired into the CRM you already use.
Do you only work with realtors and lenders? +
That's where we start, because speed-to-lead matters most in real estate and mortgage. But the exact same system works for any local business losing calls and leads after hours — so if that's you, let's talk.
Will this replace my current CRM? +
No. We build on top of the tools you already use and push leads straight into your existing CRM. We're adding the front desk — not another login you have to babysit.
I already have a website. Do I need a new one? +
Not necessarily. The front desk can bolt onto most existing sites. If your current site is slow, doesn't rank, or doesn't capture leads, we'll tell you honestly in the free audit — no pressure to rebuild.
How fast can we go live? +
Websites can turn around in days. A front desk setup is typically live within a week or two of kickoff, depending on how your calls and CRM are set up. The audit gives you an exact timeline.
