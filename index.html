What does ProyTech actually do? +
We make your website the hub of your business and wire it into everything you use — CRM, lead gen, follow-up, booking, ads, and newsletters — with an AI front desk that answers every lead in seconds. One connected system, not ten tools that don't talk.
Do you only work with realtors and lenders? +
That's where we start, because speed-to-lead and long-term nurture matter most in real estate and mortgage. But the same system works for any local business losing calls and leads — so if that's you, let's talk.
Will this replace my current CRM? +
No. We build on top of the tools you already use and push everything into your existing CRM. We're adding the front desk — not another login to babysit.
How does the newsletter actually help? +
Most of your future clients aren't ready today. A custom-branded newsletter keeps you in their inbox every month with real value — no selling — so when they're finally ready to buy or refinance, you're the name they already trust. It's the cheapest, highest-leverage follow-up there is.
How fast can we go live? +
Websites can turn around in days. A front desk setup is usually live within a week or two, depending on your calls and CRM. The free audit gives you an exact timeline — and honest answers.
